My name is Guilherme Miotto, and I am a computer vision engineer based in Germany. It is not easy to keep up with the pace that Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision progress nowadays. So, inspired by the idea of atomic habits, I decided to read at least one paper per week in this area and summarize it here. Most of the time, I read something that has just been published. But I also read older studies if the topic interests me.

If you want to contact me, you can find my info on this page. I welcome reading suggestions, corrections, high fives, etc. :-)
